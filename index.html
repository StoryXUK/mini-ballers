About Us

Introducing little ones to sport

It is never too early for little ones to be introduced to sport and that is what we do here at Mini Ballers. Through play and games, we offer a range of sporting development activities for both boys and girls, which helps children to improve their co-ordination, movement, balance and strength. Children are also introduced to the concept of listening to and following instructions, whilst learning life skills such as teamwork and friendship. This is all whilst having a tremendous amount of fun. Emphasis is placed on praise and effort at this early age, rather than overall improvement. Children will be encouraged to play openly and freely, as open-ended play encourages social and emotional development.

Research has shown that children who are introduced to sport at an early age go on to develop a more positive attitude towards health and activity and feel more motivated and confident as a result.

Frequently Asked Questions

+ How long are sessions?
Each class is 45 minutes.
+ What should my child wear?
Please make sure your child wears clothes they are comfortable in and can move freely in. Loose clothing such as dresses is not recommended.
+ What footwear is recommended?
Trainers are recommended for both children and adults.
+ What else do we need to bring?
Please bring a drink for your child.
+ Do parents/guardians/careers need to remain at sessions?
Yes- at all sessions parents/guardians/carers have the sole responsibility for your child. In the Tiddlers and Toddlers sessions, you will be required to help your child undertake the different activities.
