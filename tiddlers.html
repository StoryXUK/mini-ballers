Tiddlers

This is our youngest class. Starting at 18 months allows your child to develop their fundamental movement skills from the earliest opportunity. Classes also provide learning opportunities for body parts, numbers and colours. Whilst listening and following instructions are still very much in their early stages, repetition of key skills allows your little ones to start grasping sporting concepts in a fun and active way.
