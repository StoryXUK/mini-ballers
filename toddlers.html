Toddlers

Children at this age are more accustomed to listening to and following instructions, with the help from an adult of course! Classes continue to be centred around play and fun, which enable your little ones to use their imaginations as they take part in creative and energetic activities. Prior learning from our Mini Ballers class is built upon and children are provided with learning opportunities in addition to their physical development. For example games require children to identify the correct coloured cones and equipment to be used.
